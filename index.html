About Muscle?

Muscle is awesome. You get a 1140px layout for dem big 'ol screens, as well as a 960px layout for those stuck in the 90's. It's super awesome and comes with iPad landscape/portrait as well as iPhone landscape/portrait. This awesomeness is a result of me being a badass.

Three Core Principles

Muscle is built on three core principles:

  • A Responsive Grid that scales from 1140px to mobile: Kickass.
  • Easy to use: great starting place for all your projects.
  • Simple: It provides the most basic, beautiful styles, but is meant to be overwritten.
